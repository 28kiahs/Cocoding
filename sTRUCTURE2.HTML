Lesson 16
Descendants
Why I Like It:

Descendants shows that people who really want to change and become good can do so. It uses a great plot, great costumes, great scenery and great acting to convey that message. It also shows how a caring environment can get people to change.
The music is fantastic. Broadway legend Kristin Chenoweth plays Disney villain Maleficent, which she has described as her darkest role yet. Her song, "Evil Like Me," has been stuck in my head for a week. The songs have fantastic choreography.
The costumes are edgy. Project Runway's Kara Saun designed the costumes, and we loved them. Favorite outfit: Mal's sparkly purple coronation ballgown.
It has positive messages. The kids of the Disney Villains go to school with the kids of the Disney heroes. The villains want to get the Fairy Godmother's wand so their parents can take over the world. But let's just say that they choose a smarter path. You can use it as a jumping off point about why you don't have to fit in with the crowd.

CHARACTERS


MAL
Mal is the daughter of Malificent,the mistress of evil, and Hades, the god of the underworld. She has magic powers and the ability to shape-shift into a dragon.(The leftmost picture below shows Mal. The middle picture shows Mal with her friends: Evie, Jay, Carlos, and Ben.)Also, did you know that Mal and Ben are a couple!?

BEN
Ben is the prince of Auradon and soon becomes king after he proposes to Mal. (His picture is after Mal's.)

AUDREY
Audrey is in love with Ben, and turns into the Queen of Mean when Ben proposes to Mal. She steals Malificent's scepter, but later is defeated by Mal and Uma, and she apologizes to everyone.(Her picture fust after the one in the middle.)

UMA
Uma is Mal's enemy at first and is evil, but later Mal and Uma both join forces to defeat The Queen of Mean (Audrey). Ever since, the two of them were friends. She can turn into a huge turquoise octopus. (Her picture is the last one.)
